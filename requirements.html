Standard Requirements (All Applications)

  • Application Form:

    Purchased from the nearest OGPDPA Area Planning Office.

  • Original Survey Plan:

    Must be registered and verifiable.

  • Land Title Documents:

    Certificate of Occupancy (C of O), Deed of Assignment, or Governor's Consent. Must be duly Stamp Dutied.

  • Tax Clearance Certificate:

    Current tax clearance certificate of the applicant.

  • Site Photographs:

    Current photographs showing the state of the land.

Residential Permits

  • Architectural Drawings (5 Sets):

    Signed and sealed by an ARCON registered architect.

  • Structural Drawings (5 Sets):

    Mandatory for residential buildings with one or more suspended floors. Signed and sealed by a COREN registered structural engineer.

  • Site Layout Plan:

    Showing setbacks and drainage flow.

Commercial, Industrial & Public Buildings

  • Architectural Drawings (5 Sets):

    Complete set signed and sealed by ARCON registered architect.

  • Structural Drawings (5 Sets):

    Mandatory for all commercial buildings. Must be signed and sealed by COREN registered structural engineer.

  • Electrical Drawings (5 Sets):

    Comprehensive electrical layout and designs.

  • Mechanical Drawings (5 Sets):

    Plumbing and HVAC layouts as applicable.

  • Physical Planning Audit Report (2 Sets):

    Mandatory for audit and compliance verification.

  • Traffic Impact Assessment Report (2 Sets):

    Required to assess the impact of the development on surrounding traffic.

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA):

    Mandatory for factories, shopping complexes, filling stations, and public institutions.

  • Soil Test Report:

    Required for technical verification of ground conditions.
