Professional Practices & Standards

The Nigerian Institute of Town Planners (NITP) is the leading professional body for urban and regional planners in Nigeria, dedicated to the following core practices:

  • Fostering Professional Excellence:

    Upholding the highest ethical and professional standards to instil trust in the planning profession.

  • Policy & Advocacy:

    Driving forward-thinking policies to address urbanization challenges and advocating for sustainable planning at all levels of government.

  • Urban Design & Management:

    Organizing and managing the physical development of cities to create sustainable, functional, and aesthetically pleasing environments.

  • Capacity Building & Research:

    Continuous education and research to solve complex spatial and environmental issues for the benefit of quality living.

  • Regulation of Standards:

    Ensuring that registered town planners adhere to the code of conduct and building codes set by the institute and government authorities.
